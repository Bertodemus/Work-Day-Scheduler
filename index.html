Welcome, to ORCHESTRATE!

ORCHESTRATE is a simple, yet sleek, "work-day" planner that grants you the ability to assign each hour block with a task or an appointment that you would like to accomplish within that time frame.

There are three colors to indicate the status of each hour block:

           - CURRENT HOUR

           - FUTURE HOUR

           - PAST HOUR (text entry is disabled)

ORCHESTRATE, upon initialization, will open to the hour block that corresponds with your current time.

Each hour block has a header that indicates the hour and whether or not there is an entry for this block.
